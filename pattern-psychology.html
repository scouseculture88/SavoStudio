Research Insights

Military Camouflage Studies

Research shows that irregular, high-contrast patterns are most effective at disrupting object recognition at distances over 100 meters.

Source: Military Psychology Research, 2019

Urban Pattern Perception

Geometric patterns with right angles create feelings of order and control, while organic patterns evoke relaxation and natural connection.

Source: Environmental Psychology Journal, 2020

Digital Pattern Effects

High-contrast digital patterns can cause eye strain after 15 minutes but increase alertness and focus in short bursts.

Source: Digital Design Health Study, 2021

Color Pattern Combinations

Green-dominant patterns reduce cortisol levels by 12% compared to red-dominant patterns, which increase alertness by 18%.

Source: Color Psychology Research, 2022
